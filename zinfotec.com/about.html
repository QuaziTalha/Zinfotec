ABOUT US

ZinfoTec Technologies PVT. LTD.

Welcome to ZinfoTec Technologies PVT. LTD.., a leading professional LED display screen development and supply company. With years of experience in this field, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in Maharashtra since our inception in 2014. Our comprehensive infrastructure includes an assembling unit, workshop, dedicated marketing team, and a commitment to providing excellent after-sales support to our valued customers.

Previously, we were recognized as a distinguished importer, supplier, and service provider of LED display screen systems across India. However, our continuous pursuit of success has led us to establish our own state-of-the-art assembling plant in Maharashtra. This strategic move has allowed us to streamline our operations and deliver even greater value to our customers. Through the concerted efforts of our company and staff, coupled with strong collaborations and cooperation, our products have gained significant traction in the developed areas of the Indian market. We actively engage with talented individuals and dealers to foster mutual business growth and contribute to the overall advancement of the LED display system industry.

ZinfoTec Technologies PVT. LTD.. is widely recognized as a reputable dealer and now a prominent supplier of world-class LED Display Systems. Moreover, we pride ourselves on being an eminent service provider, offering a wide range of related services that ensure utmost customer satisfaction. Our commitment extends beyond the point of sale as we take full responsibility for all our products for a period of one year. Additionally, we provide lifetime maintenance at competitive prices, making sure that our customers receive ongoing support and value for their investment.

To enhance the overall customer experience, we offer an exceptional after-sales service package. This includes free training sessions, providing convenience and empowering our clients with the necessary knowledge to utilize our products effectively. We also offer lifetime free consultations through our online support platform, ensuring that our customers have access to expert guidance whenever they need it. As part of our commitment to quality assurance, we conduct periodic inspections of our sold products, ensuring that they continue to meet the highest standards of performance and reliability.

At ZinfoTec Technologies PVT. LTD.., we take immense pride in our reputation as a trusted dealer and supplier of world-class LED Display Systems. Our dedication to providing exceptional products, comprehensive services, and ongoing support has earned us a wide base of satisfied clients spread across India. We look forward to serving you with our cutting-edge technology, competitive prices, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
